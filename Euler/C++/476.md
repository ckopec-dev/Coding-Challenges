I'll solve Euler Problem 476, which involves finding the maximum area of a triangle formed by three points on a circle.

## Problem Understanding

Euler Problem 476 asks us to find the maximum area of a triangle formed by three points on a circle of radius 1, where the triangle has a perimeter of 3.

## Solution Approach
